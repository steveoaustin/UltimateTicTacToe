Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe
How to play:
Ultimate tic-tac-toe is a game that combines the fun fast pace of tic-tac-toe with a more strategic element, like chess. Your goal is to get three in a row in the large board by winning individual sub-boards, but be careful, your move in each board dictates where your opponent can move next. For example, if you play in the top left square of a board, your opponent will be able to play anywhere in the top left board on their turn.

You will play each turn in the board highlighted in purple. The computer will move automatically after you do.
